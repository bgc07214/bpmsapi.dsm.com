After receiving the responsibility as the Minister of Agriculture, the Minister of Agriculture informed about the plan of meeting with farmers across the country to increase the production of crops. Md. Abdus Shaheed. As part of this, he held the first courtyard meeting.<\/p>\n

About five hundred farmers were present in the meeting held in the courtyard of farmer Indu Bhushan Pal Niru of Noagaon village of Srimangal upazila on Saturday morning. Agriculture Minister asked the farmers why the land of Aman paddy harvesting village is still fallow.<\/p>\n

The minister directed the local agriculture department to formulate an action plan within the next 50 days on how to bring cultivable fallow land under cultivation in Moulvibazar district, what crops can be grown on which land. He said, there is a lot of cultivable land. Now is the Boro season, but all the land here is lying fallow after harvesting the Aman paddy. We have to find ways to utilize these lands.<\/p>\n

At this time, the minister ordered BADC to take action plan to solve the irrigation problem. He said that more agricultural machinery will be given at subsidized prices to solve the labor crisis.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
