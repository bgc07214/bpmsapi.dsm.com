A Sharjah-bound Air Arabia flight turned back 2 hours after leaving Chittagong Shah Amanat Airport due to a mechanical fault. The plane landed safely at the airport at 9:35 pm on Saturday.<\/p>\n

Earlier, the Sharjah-bound flight left Chittagong Airport with 149 passengers on Saturday around 7:30 pm. After the flight approached Kolkata, the aircraft experienced an electrical problem. <\/p>\n

Chittagong Shah Amanat International Airport Director Group Captain Taslim Ahmed confirmed this information.<\/p>\n

Group Captain Taslim said, after the mechanical problem, the pilot brought the plane back quickly without taking any risk. It is now at the airport. It was removed from the airport after landing. Mechanical troubleshooting is in progress. Passengers are accommodated in airport lounges and served meals.
