It is known that the procession was led by Johnny Hasan Labu, President of Municipal Jubo League, at Singra Bus Stand area at 11:30 am on Friday. In the viral video, it can be seen that most of the people who took part in the procession were carrying different types of indigenous weapons. Some of them had ramadas, some iron rods, Chinese axes and hansua. Half a hundred activists took part in the march led by Labu wearing a helmet. Among them, Bayjid Hossain, president of Ward No. 9 of Municipal Jubo League, Anwar Hossain Saudagar, former Publicity and Publication Secretary of Municipal Volunteer League, Barat Ali Saudagar and Jahangir Saudagar, Jubo League workers, were seen holding weapons. The office of the CNG-powered Auto-Rickshaw Owners Association was vandalized while chanting slogans. Panic spread in the area when people openly marched with arms on the Natore-Bogra highway.<\/p>\n

Singra Municipal Jubo League President Johnny Hasan Labu said that the administration could not stop extortion in public transport for 15 years. Therefore, this march has been done with the traders with weapons to stop the levy.<\/p>\n

Meanwhile, Ranju Mondal, general secretary of the CNG-driven auto-rickshaw owners association, did not give any answer when asked about the reason for not filing a case in the case of office vandalism. However, he said, the incident happened due to a misunderstanding. <\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
